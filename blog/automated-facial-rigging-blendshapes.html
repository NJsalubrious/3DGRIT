Blendshape Generation

▸

Instead of manually sculpting every expression, we can mathematically derive subsets of expressions from a single "extreme" pose. This script unwraps an entire base mesh state across 52+ delta maps, ensuring absolute 1:1 symmetry where needed and isolated asymmetric splits natively within Blender. No third-party tools required.

Driving Bones with Shapes

▸

Pure vertex manipulation scales terribly across engines. We instead invert the relationship: the generated blendshapes act as targets for bone positional data. The geometry follows the bone, but the bone's trajectory is driven by the shape key difference.

This keeps your skeleton completely deterministic. The bones remain the authority, while the sculpt provides the envelope.
