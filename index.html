Modular tools designed to accelerate your pipeline from Blender, to Unity, to the Web.

1. 3D GRIT Auto-Rigger

The ultimate modular framework. Build complex bipeds, quadrupeds, and custom rigs in seconds.

2. MayaForBlender

Seamlessly bridge your workflow. Bring industry-standard Maya behaviors and shortcuts directly into Blender.
